Frequently Asked Questions

Some of your questions are answered

How does pricing work?

Our pricing is based on project complexity, duration, and any specific requirements you have. We offer transparent and customized quotes for each project.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

We offer a structured revision process, typically allowing 2-3 rounds of revisions per project, depending on the initial agreement.

What do I need to provide?

We ask clients to provide any brand guidelines, reference materials, scripts, or specific visual preferences before we begin.

What software do you use?

We primarily use industry-standard tools such as Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve for video editing and color correction.

What is your availability for ongoing projects?

We are available for ongoing projects and can discuss availability based on your project's needs and timeline.
